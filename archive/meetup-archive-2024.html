SUN 14.01.2024: "Workshop Live Coding with Mercury" @CCU, Hof van Cartesius

Time: 13h00 - 17h00

Price: €40 (€20 for students)

Location: CCU, Hof van Cartesius, Vlampijpstraat 84, Utrecht

Join this crash-course into the exciting world of live coding music. No previous knowledge of programming required, only a desire to express yourself creatively! During this workshop you will learn to create electronic music with computercode and algorithms! For this we use the programming language Mercury.

TUE 16.01.2024: "NL_CL Live Coding Meetup" @CCU, Hof van Cartesius

Time: 20h00 - 22h00

Price: Pay what you can €2.50 - €7.50 - €12.50 (includes a drink)

Location: CCU, Hof van Cartesius, Vlampijpstraat 84, Utrecht

Join us for the first live coding meetup of the new year on January 16th! This time we have a guest from TOPLAP Barcelona, Lina Bautista a.k.a. Linalab, who will present her work as a sound live coder and how she combines the laptop and modular synthesizers to make music. After that we have an open stage. Anyone is welcome to take the HDMI cable and present their work-in-progress, display the issues they’ve been running into, and show their solutions! Please reach out beforehand if you're interested in presenting something. We will end the night with a from-scratch 9-minute live coding session, jamming with live coded music and visuals!

Everyone is welcome to join. Come and hang out!

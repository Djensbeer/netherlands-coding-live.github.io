Upcoming Events 🗓


TUE 12.09.2023: "Live Coding Meetup" @CCU, Hof van Cartesius

Time: 20h00 - 22h00

Location: CCU, Hof van Cartesius, Utrecht

Join us for a collab meetup with our friends from the Netherlands Coding Live (NL_CL) community on September 12th. We will get together to talk about live coding. Anyone is welcome to take the HDMI cable and present their work-in-progress, display the issues they’ve been running into, and show their solutions! We will end the night with an open-stage live code jam, where we will be jamming with live coded music and visuals.

Everyone is welcome to join the conversation, no previous coding experience is necessary. Come and hang out!
